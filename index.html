The Ownership Model

A revolutionary approach to homeownership in Bangladesh.

Your Path to Homeownership, Simplified.

We are proud to introduce a first-of-its-kind lease-to-own model in Bangladesh, designed to make homeownership a reality for the emerging middle class.

  • ‚úîZero Down Payment: Secure your home without the burden of a large upfront payment.
  • ‚úîFrom Renter to Owner: Your monthly payments act as both occupancy and equity contributions, turning long-term tenants into homeowners.
  • ‚úîFinancial Inclusion: This model reduces dependency on developer debt and aligns with national affordability goals.
  • ‚úîSecure and Bankable: The entire structure is fully mortgage-backed, ensuring security for our financial partners.

A Self-Financed, Risk-Mitigated Development Cycle

Our unique development model is designed to be debt-free and self-sustaining.

  1. 1

    Buyer Commits

    The cycle begins when a buyer signs an agreement and pays the first 6 months of EMIs upfront.

  2. 2

    Construction Starts

    This initial capital is used to begin construction on a small, 2.05% phase of the total project.

  3. 3

    70-80% Completion

    At 70% completion, cost is recovered. At 80%, IFIC Bank disburses the loan.

  4. 4

    Unit Delivered & Cycle Repeats

    The land payment is made, the buyer receives their unit, and surplus funds begin the next cycle.
